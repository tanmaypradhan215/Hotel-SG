ABOUT US

Welcome to Sri Guru, where tradition meets taste! Located in the heart of Brahmapur, Sri Guru is more
than just a restaurant; it's a celebration of authentic flavors, warmth, and a deep love for food. Inspired by the rich culinary
heritage of India, we take pride in serving dishes that reflect the vibrant culture, rich spices, and timeless recipes passed down through generations.

At Sri Guru, every meal is a tribute to our roots, cooked with fresh ingredients, aromatic spices, and a passion for perfection. Whether you’re in the mood for a comforting family dinner, a special celebration, or just a quick bite, we promise a dining experience that feels like home.

Thank you for choosing Sri Guru. We invite you to embark on a flavorful journey with us, savoring the essence of India, one bite at a time.
