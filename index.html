ABOUT US

Welcome to Sri Guru, where tradition meets taste! Located in the heart of Brahmapur, Sri Guru is more than just a restaurant; it's a celebration of authentic flavors, warmth, and a deep love for food. Inspired by the rich culinary
heritage of India, we take pride in serving dishes that reflect the vibrant culture, rich spices, and timeless recipes passed down through generations.

Thank you for choosing Sri Guru. We invite you to embark on a flavorful journey with us, savoring the essence of India, one bite at a time.
